What it does

Tap to open a bin

Hold your iPhone to a tub's NFC tag and Cubby opens straight to its contents — even from the lock screen, with the app closed.

Scan the QR too

No NFC, or hands full on iPad? The printed QR label and a short human code (BIN-12) get you to the same place.

Add items by camera

Point the camera at an item and on-device Vision suggests a name. A sharpness check rejects blurry shots before they're saved.

Scan things in and out

Open a bin, then add, remove, or move items. Quantities update as you go and every change is logged to an activity feed.

Search across everything

One search field spans item names, notes, bins, and racks — find the holiday lights without opening a single lid.

Print your own labels

Lay out QR labels for whole racks at once and print to an Avery sheet or a roll printer over AirPrint. Tag once, find forever.

Where is it?

From a search hit, see the bin's spot on a rack map — then tap its tag to confirm you're standing at the right one.

Collections & smart filters

Group items into Collections that span bins, tag them, and narrow with filters: category, location, in-stock, has-a-photo.

Lend it, get it back

Mark a tool as lent with a due date and get a reminder to chase it. See what's out, what's overdue, and what you borrowed.

Never run out

Set a par level on consumables. "Running low" shows what's below par and how many more you need — then share a shopping note.

A full timeline

Every add, move, lend, and scan is logged — a house-wide activity feed, a per-item history, and a monthly recap.

Expiry & warranty reminders new

Give perishables an expiry and gear a warranty date. Cubby reminds you before the milk turns or the coverage lapses — even for something due today.

Snap a receipt new

Photograph a receipt and Cubby reads the total, purchase date, and serial number to pre-fill an item's value — on device, always yours to review.

Photos, receipts & manuals new

Attach extra photos plus PDF receipts and manuals to any item, kept as files on your device alongside the item.

Your own fields new

Add custom attributes to any item — Voltage → 20V, Model → X200 — without a rigid template.

Add many at once new

Capture a shelf in one shot and review the suggested items together, then add the whole batch in a tap.

Find from iOS Search new

Your items surface in the iPhone's own Spotlight search — type a name and jump straight into the right bin.

Insurance report & backup new

Export a tidy PDF inventory for insurance, or a CSV / JSON backup you can re-import any time — restoring twice never duplicates a thing.

Trash with undo new

Removed something by mistake? Items go to Trash and restore in a tap — reminders, photos, and all — instead of vanishing for good.

Private by architecture

No account to create, nothing uploaded, no analytics. Your inventory lives on your device. Optional sync uses your own iCloud.

How it works

Tag your racks & bins

Stick an NFC tag and a printed QR label on each rack and tub. Cubby writes the tag for you in one tap.

Fill them in

Open a bin in the app and add what's inside — snap a photo, accept or edit the suggested name, set a quantity.

Find it later

Tap a tag, scan a QR, or search by name. Cubby tells you which bin on which rack — no more digging through every lid.

Requirements: iPhone or iPad running iOS 26 or later. Tap-to-open uses NFC (iPhone); QR scanning and short codes work everywhere, including iPad.
